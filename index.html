Perspectives of a Semester — Ten Years Later

An interactive visual novel inspired by Zadie Smith's Swing Time. Experience an exchange semester in Taiwan from four different viewpoints and see how they clash ten years later.

Guangfu Campus — June

June is scorching hot on the Guangfu campus. The air feels heavy and oppressive, the sound of cicadas fills the background while people hurry across the campus, eager to spend as little time as possible under the blazing sun and to reach the air-conditioned buildings.

On that day, the university is hosting a congress. All former students from the past ten years who are currently in Taiwan have been invited to gather and reconnect.
